About Us


Our team at Social Eminence can offer you community building, access to new demographics and a competitive edge in the digital age. If you're not on social media, you're not relevant. Thats where we can help. Social media requires businesses to start thinking like small-town shop owners. This means taking the long view and avoiding short-term benchmarks to gauge progress. It means allowing the personality, heart and soul of your business to be 100% reflective of how you wish to be percieved by the public.

Consider us your secret weapon and driving force of your online presence. We can help you every step of the way from getting Facebook verified, designing size-relative images for Facebook, Twitter, Google+ cover photos, set up promotions abd contests, schedule content and more.

What sets us apart at Social Eminence? We like numbers. We can show you quantifiable results through social media analytics, and we can help you set and reach realistic goals for your business. Contact us today and see how we can create a social media marketing strategy that works for you.
